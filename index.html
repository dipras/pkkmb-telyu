PKKMB at Telkom University Surabaya: A Personal Experience

I recently had the opportunity to participate in the 3-day PKKMB (Pengenalan Kehidupan Kampus bagi Mahasiswa Baru) program at Telkom University Surabaya. It was an insightful introduction to university life, filled with valuable information and engaging activities.

Throughout the event, we were introduced to a variety of excellent speakers, each offering unique insights into what it means to be a university student. Their advice and experiences gave us a clearer picture of the challenges and opportunities ahead.

The program also provided us with a solid understanding of the basic principles of student life, from academic expectations to campus culture. It was a great way to prepare ourselves mentally and practically for the journey we are about to embark on.

One of the highlights for me was the interaction with the facilitators. The senior students were not only knowledgeable but also very approachable and fun to be around. Their enthusiasm made the experience much more enjoyable and helped create a positive and supportive environment for us newcomers.

Overall, PKKMB was a fantastic introduction to university life, and I feel more confident and excited to begin my academic journey at Telkom University Surabaya.
